During my interview with Wilson Onyango, the owner of Kalare Enterprises, I learned about his growing online electronics business. The company specializes in selling a variety of electronic products, including laptops, smartphones, gaming accessories, and other modern technology devices.

Wilson explained that his main customers are households and families looking for reliable electronics, students who need devices for learning, gamers interested in high-performance accessories, and technology enthusiasts who enjoy keeping up with the latest gadgets.

He expressed the need for a simple, attractive, and professional website that would help customers easily view available products and make informed purchasing decisions. The website should clearly display product names, brands, prices, warranty details, shipping and delivery information, and contact details. Wilson also wanted customers to be able to quickly reach him for inquiries, orders, and after-sales support.

The overall goal of the website is to increase the business's online presence, improve customer engagement, and make it easier for people across Kenya to access quality electronic products from Kalare Enterprises.
